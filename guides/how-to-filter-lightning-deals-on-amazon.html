How to Filter Lightning Deals on Amazon

Quick answer: Use Deal Shopper Pro’s one‑click filters to zero‑in on Prime‑eligible Lightning Deals by price, rating or discount—no URL hacks required.

Step 1 – Filter Lightning Deals in one click

  1. Open Deal Shopper Pro.
  2. In Step 1, type Lightning Deals.
  3. In Step 2, enter your desired minimum and maximum price.
  4. Use the dropdown menus in Step 3 (rating, discount %, sort order) to fine‑tune your search, and optionally add brand filters in Step 4.
  5. In Step 5, tick Lightning Deals and any other filters you want to include, then click the blue View Filtered Results button. You’ll be redirected to Amazon with every result already filtered by your price range, rating, discount %, and any other options you picked—saving you clicks and hiding out‑of‑stock or irrelevant items.

Step 2 – Mobile vs desktop

The Lightning Deals list hides most filters on small screens. After the redirect:

  • Desktop: click the dropdown in Step 3 to pick rating, discount %, or sort order—everything stays visible on the page.
  • Mobile: tap Step 3; a full‑screen picker slides up with the same rating, discount %, and sort options. Make your choices, tap Done, then tick Lightning Deals in Step 5 and press View Filtered Results.

Deal Shopper Pro passes the same parameters, so results are identical.

FAQ

Can I sort Lightning Deals by highest discount?

Yes. Inside Deal Shopper Pro choose Sort → Discount % before clicking “View Results.”

Why do Lightning Deals disappear?

Each deal has limited stock and a timer (usually 4–12 hours). When either runs out, Amazon removes the listing.
