Step-by-Step: Filter Amazon Lightning Deals

1) Open the Lightning Deals finder

Go to the dedicated page and pick a quick preset or start the full guided search.

Open Lightning Deals

2) Add keywords or choose a category

Use product keywords, an ASIN/ISBN/UPC, or select a category to cast a wider net.

3) Set your price, rating & sort

  • Minimum rating filter (e.g., 4★+)
  • Price range (e.g., $15–$50)
  • Sort by price-asc or review-rank

4) Turn on Lightning Deals only

This narrows results to active time-limited offers. If nothing matches right now, try a broader search or check again later.

5) Add optional filters (highly recommended)

  • Prime-only
  • Brand include
  • Coupons available
  • Shipping & availability
  • Eco & ethical options
  • Gift ideas & special categories

When you’re ready, click View Results — your filtered Lightning Deals will open directly on Amazon in a new tab.
