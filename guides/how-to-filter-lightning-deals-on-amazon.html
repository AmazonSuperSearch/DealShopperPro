How Lightning Deals Work

Lightning Deals are limited‑time promotions that live on Amazon’s Today’s Deals page. Each deal runs for a set number of hours—or until stock sells out. Once it’s gone, the offer vanishes from search results.

Method 1 – Copy‑Paste URL Filters

  1. Open Amazon Lightning Deals.
  2. Add a price filter. Example: limit to $10–40:
    https://www.amazon.com/gp/goldbox?&p_36=1000-4000
  3. Add a discount‑percent filter (≥40 % off):
    …&discount=40-100
  4. Combine filters:
    …?p_36=1000-4000&discount=40-100&sort=discount-desc-rank

Method 3 – Mobile vs Desktop

On Amazon’s mobile app the URL bar isn’t visible, so URL hacks don’t work. Use Deal Shopper Pro in your mobile browser or:

  • Go to Deals ▶ Lightning Deals
  • Tap the filter funnel icon
  • Slide toggles for price or discount

FAQ

Can I sort Lightning Deals by highest discount? Yes—append &sort=discount-desc-rank to the URL, or click the “Sort by Discount” toggle in Deal Shopper Pro.

Why do Lightning Deals disappear? They’re time‑boxed and stock‑limited; when the timer or inventory ends, Amazon removes them from the list.
