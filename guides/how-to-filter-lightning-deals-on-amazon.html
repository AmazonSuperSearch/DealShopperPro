Step-by-Step: Smarter Deal Filtering

  1. Open the Amazon Lightning Deals finder and browse current offers.
  2. Enter a keyword or category to target the type of product you want.
  3. Set your price range and choose a minimum rating (e.g., 4★+).
  4. Turn on the “Lightning Deals only” filter so expired or unrelated items are excluded.
  5. Enhance your search with extra options like Prime-only deals, coupons, brand filters, and shipping preferences.
  6. Click “View Results” and you’ll be taken directly to Amazon with your filters applied.
