Privacy Policy

Last updated – 21 June 2025

1. Who We Are

Deal Shopper Pro (“we,” “our,” “us”) is an independent search front-end that helps you find Amazon deals more efficiently. We are not part of Amazon.com, Inc. or its subsidiaries.

2. What Data We Collect (Very Little)

  • No accounts or login required.
  • No cookies are set by us.
  • No search data is stored: all filters and queries run directly in your browser and are never saved to a server.
  • Affiliate clicks: when you click a result, you are redirected to Amazon using our Associate tag. Amazon may set its own cookies as described in their Privacy Notice.

3. How We Use Data

We do not collect personal data. There is no tracking, no personalization, and no advertising profiles.

  • We do not store or analyze your searches.
  • We do not use third-party analytics platforms.
  • We do not serve retargeted ads or share information with marketers.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Amazon Associates: Amazon handles any transactions, shipping, and customer support. You are subject to Amazon’s terms and privacy policy.
  • Hosting: This site is hosted via GitHub Pages and delivered through Cloudflare, which may log basic information (IP, browser type) for security and caching purposes. These logs are not connected to you personally and are not stored or analyzed by us.

5. Your Rights

  • GDPR (EU/UK): You may request access or deletion of any personal data we might hold (we normally hold none).
  • CCPA/CPRA (California): You can request data disclosure or opt out of sale (we do not sell data).

Email privacy@dealshopperpro.com to exercise your rights.

6. Children’s Privacy

This site is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child has submitted personal data, please contact us and we will delete it.

7. Updates

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted here with a revised date. Please check back to stay informed.
