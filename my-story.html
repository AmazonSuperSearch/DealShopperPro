I Lost Everything—Then Built a Free Tool to Help Millions Shop Smarter on Amazon

By Duane Romualdo | Creator of DealShopperPro.com

There are moments in life when everything changes. For me, that moment came more than once.

At 15, I lost my father to cancer.

At 19, I was in a coma — and woke up completely paralyzed on the left side of my body.
It wasn’t permanent, but the road back was brutal. I was determined to recover. I spent every day pushing myself — from wheelchair to walker, from walker to walking again. I forced my body to fight, even when it didn’t want to.
Today, I’m proud to say I regained a lot. But the truth is, I’ll never be 100%. The left side of my body is still weaker, slower, less coordinated. And that’s okay. I kept going anyway.

Each time, I rebuilt. But after Hurricane Ian hit Fort Myers Beach, FL, I lost everything I owned — and even a few good friends who didn’t survive the storm. Starting over wasn’t just emotional — it was physical, financial, and digital. I relocated to Pennsylvania in 2023 with no resources, no home, and no clear path forward.

But I did have one thing: a relentless determination to make something work online.

I became obsessed with AI — not just the hype, but the real potential. I didn’t have a team. I didn’t have a budget. But I had an idea and ChatGPT.

The Problem That Sparked Everything

Amazon is where we all shop — but have you ever tried to find the exact product you need?

The filters barely work. You can’t sort by Prime-only + coupon + price range + rating + color + discount. You can’t even easily find Lightning Deals or brand-specific sales without digging through pages of junk.

I kept thinking: Why is it so hard to search Amazon properly?

So I built the solution.

What I Created — With No Money and No Code Team

DealShopperPro.com is the tool I wish existed. It’s a free Amazon deal-finder that lets anyone filter products with precision:

  • Want only Prime-eligible items? Easy.
  • Looking for Lightning Deals under $25 with free shipping? One click.
  • Need a specific brand, size, rating, or coupon combo? Done.

The site uses Amazon’s public affiliate structure, meaning I earn a small commission if someone buys through it — but it costs users nothing. No logins. No popups. No gimmicks. Just powerful search.

And it works — even on mobile.

Built With AI. Run By One Person. Helping Thousands.

This wasn’t built in Silicon Valley. I didn’t raise VC money.

I built it from a tiny room, using ChatGPT and determination.
