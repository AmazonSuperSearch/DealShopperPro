How Amazon Lightning Deals Work

Lightning Deals are limited‑time, limited‑quantity promotions that appear throughout the day on Amazon. Each deal has a percentage‑claimed bar; when it hits 100% or the timer expires, the deal ends. Some offers are Prime‑exclusive, others are open to all shoppers. Because inventory moves quickly, the fastest way to win is to filter for the exact items you want instead of scrolling through thousands of listings.

  • Time window: Deals rotate all day; many last a few hours or until stock is gone.
  • Quantity: If a deal is sold out, you may see Join Waitlist—still worth it for high‑demand items.
  • Prime benefits: Many Lightning Deals stack with Prime free shipping; some are Prime Lightning Deals only.
  • Coupons: Some listings include an extra digital coupon—our tool surfaces these so you get the best Amazon lightning deals today.
