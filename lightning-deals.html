How Amazon Lightning Deals Work

Lightning Deals are limited-time, limited-quantity promotions that appear throughout the day on Amazon. Each deal has a percentage-claimed bar; when it hits 100% or the timer expires, the deal ends.

Set a realistic range to avoid filtering out good results. Deep discounts on premium items are uncommon, so choose a sensible cap.

Set minimum 4-star rating to avoid low-quality items. For discount percentage, 20%+ filters out minor deals and focuses on significant savings.

4

Choose Preferred Brands

This step dramatically improves result quality. If you're shopping for electronics, adding "Apple, Samsung, Sony" eliminates unknown brands.

6

Review Your Results

You should now see only Lightning Deals that match ALL your criteria. If you see too few results, remove the brand filter first, then adjust the discount percentage.

How do I find Lightning Deals on Amazon?

What time do new Lightning Deals appear?

Are Lightning Deals actually good deals?

Most Lightning Deals offer genuine savings, but some discounts can be misleading. Check recent price history and favor established brands.

Deals typically last a few hours, and popular electronics can sell out quickly. Listings with multiple size/color variations often last longer.
