Help & How-To

How to Filter by Price Range

Use the Minimum Price and Maximum Price fields in Step 2 to narrow results to within your budget. Enter values and hit "Search Amazon" to see only items in your desired price bracket.

Finding Coupons on Amazon

In Step 5, toggle Coupons Available to display only listings with active coupon codes. Combine this with a discount percentage filter to find deep savings.

Using Boolean Operators

Enable Boolean Operators in Step 7 and use AND, OR, or NOT in your query to include or exclude terms. Example: wireless AND earbuds NOT cheap.

Applying Brand Filters

In Step 4, type brand names (e.g. Sony, LG) and optionally check "Match only these brands" to restrict results to those manufacturers.
