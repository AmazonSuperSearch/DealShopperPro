    This comprehensive Help & How-To guide has been developed by the Amazon Super Search Team, who specialize in building advanced Amazon deal-finding filters. Inside, you’ll find step-by-step instructions for every feature: from sorting by price drops to uncovering hidden coupons. Bookmark this page as your go-to resource for maximizing savings on Amazon.

    How to Find the Best Deals on Amazon with Advanced Filters

    Use a combination of price range filters (Step 2), customer ratings (Step 3), and Prime/Lightning Deal checkboxes (Step 5) to surface Amazon’s top bargains quickly. Adjust “Sort By” to “Price: Low → High” and set a minimum discount threshold for deeper savings.

    How to Find Under-$20 Prime-Eligible Lightning Deals on Amazon

    Use our Lightning Deals filter in Step 5 and set your price range to below $20 in Step 2 to surface only affordable, Prime-eligible limited-time offers.

    🧠 How to Use Power-User Tools - This section gives you extra control to target exactly what you want—like using “search magic.” Here’s how:

    • Enable Boolean Operators
      Check the box and then use AND, OR, NOT in your main search.
    • Field Filters
      Type things like field-brand:Sony to only show that brand.
    • ASIN / ISBN Lookup
      Paste a product’s ASIN or ISBN (e.g. B08N5WRWNW) to go directly to it.

    💸 How to Use Deal-Hunter Goodies - Spot real bargains and coupon opportunities using past price trends and open-box deals:

    • Discount from 90-Day Avg
      Type a percentage (e.g. “25”) to see items at least that much below their three-month avg price.
    • Coupon-Clippable Only
      Check to show only products with a visible Amazon coupon at checkout.
    • Warehouse / Open-Box
      Check to include gently used or open-box items sold by Amazon at a discount.
    • Discount from 30/60/90-Day Avg
      Enter percentages to compare against one- or two-month averages.
    • Max Daily Price Volatility
      Set a threshold (e.g. “5”) to hide listings whose price jumps wildly day-to-day.
