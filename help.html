About This Page

This comprehensive Help & How-To guide has been developed by the Amazon Super Search Team, who specialize in building advanced Amazon deal-finding filters. Inside, you’ll find step-by-step instructions for every feature: from sorting by price drops to uncovering hidden coupons. Bookmark this page as your go-to resource for maximizing savings on Amazon.

How to Find the Best Deals on Amazon with Advanced Filters

Use a combination of price range filters (Step 2), customer ratings (Step 3), and Prime/Lightning Deal checkboxes (Step 5) to surface Amazon’s top bargains quickly. Adjust “Sort By” to “Price: Low → High” and set a minimum discount threshold for deeper savings.

How to Find Under-$20 Prime-Eligible Lightning Deals on Amazon

Use our Lightning Deals filter in Step 5 and set your price range to below $20 in Step 2 to surface only affordable, Prime-eligible limited-time offers.

How to Exclude Out-of-Stock Items in Your Amazon Search Results

Check the In Stock box in Step 5 to remove all unavailable listings, so you only see products ready to ship immediately.

How to Uncover Hidden Amazon Coupon Codes Before Checkout

Toggle Coupons in Step 5 and look for the clipped coupon icon—this reveals items with hidden discount codes you can clip before purchasing.

How to Sort Amazon Results by 90-Day Price Drop Percentage

Use the Deal-Hunter Goodies section’s Discount from 90-Day Avg Price field to filter for items that dropped the most in the past three months.

How to Find High-Quality Open-Box Electronics Under $30 on Amazon

In Deal-Hunter Goodies, check Warehouse / Open-Box and set your max price to under $30 to locate budget-friendly, like-new electronics.

How to Search Amazon for Books with Free Prime Delivery

Select Prime Only and filter category to Books to see literature delivered free with your Prime membership.

How to Locate Vegan & Cruelty-Free Beauty Products Under $25 on Amazon

Use the Eco-Friendly toggle along with Cruelty-Free, set category to Beauty, and cap price at $25 for ethical skincare finds.

How to Exclude International Sellers & See US-Only Listings on Amazon

In Step 2, choose USD and in Deal-Hunter Goodies check Shipped by Amazon to limit results to US inventory.

How to Track Price Drops on Amazon Wish List Items

Add items to your Wish List, then use our Price Tracker integration to monitor and filter for desired discount thresholds.
