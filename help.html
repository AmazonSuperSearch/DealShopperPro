How to Filter Amazon Deals – FAQs & Tips

🛠️ General Use

How do I use Deal Shopper Pro to find Amazon deals?

Use the 6-step form to start your product search with keywords or identifiers, then apply filters like price range, discount %, minimum rating, and brand. You can also apply optional extras like “Prime Only,” “Lightning Deals,” and “In Stock” — then click “View Filtered Results” to open Amazon with all filters applied in real time.

Is Deal Shopper Pro really free to use?

Yes! It’s completely free, no sign-ups or subscriptions required. It helps you quickly find Amazon discounts.

Do I need an Amazon account to use this search tool?

No Amazon account is needed to use the tool. You’ll only need one if you decide to purchase an item from Amazon. It serves as an effective Amazon filter tool.

Is this an official Amazon product or app?

No. Deal Shopper Pro is an independent tool. We’re part of the Amazon Associates Program but not affiliated with Amazon. Think of it as a smart deal finder for Amazon.

What is a Lightning Deal and how do I filter for them?

Lightning Deals are limited-time, limited-quantity offers. Simply check the “⚡ Lightning Deals” box in the filters to show only these specials. You can search Amazon by price and rating with ease.

🔍 Filtering Products

How can I filter Amazon products by price range and rating?

Use the Step 2 and Step 3 filters on the homepage to set a price range and minimum star rating.

Can I search only for items with active coupons?

Yes! Check the box labeled “🏷️ Coupons” or “Coupon-Clippable Only” in the filter options.

How do I find Prime-eligible or FBA-only items?

Use the “🤩 Prime Only” and “📦 Fulfilled by Amazon” checkboxes to refine your results.

Is there a way to see only in-stock items or products with free shipping?

Yes. Just check “📦 In Stock” and/or “🚚 Free Shipping” in the extra filters section.

Can I search for products from specific brands only?

Yes. Use the “Include Brands” field and optionally check “Match only these brands.”

🔒 Privacy & Access

Do I need to create an account or log in?

No login or account is ever required to use Deal Shopper Pro.

Is my personal data tracked or saved when I use this tool?

No personal data is stored or collected. We don’t use tracking cookies.

Does Deal Shopper Pro collect cookies or browsing history?

No. This tool does not collect your browsing history or personal data.

🎯 Advanced Features

Can I sort Amazon results by customer review rating or newest arrivals?

Yes. Use the “Sort By” dropdown to choose from relevance, rating, newest, or price order.

Is there a voice search option for easier searching?

Yes. Tap the 🎤 microphone icon next to the search bar to start voice search (on supported browsers).

♻️ Niche & Ethical Shopping

How do I filter Amazon products for eco-friendly or vegan options?

Enable checkboxes like ♻️ Eco-Friendly, 🌱 Vegan, or 🌿 Organic in Step 5 filters.

Can I find allergy-friendly, cruelty-free, or low EMF products?

Yes. Just enable the relevant filters like 🤧 Allergy-Friendly, 🐰 Cruelty-Free, or 📶 Low EMF.

Where can I search for gifts for specific people (e.g. Dad, Mom, Kids)?

Use the themed filters in Step 5: 🎀 Gifts for Her, 👨 Gifts for Dad, 🧒 Gifts for Kids, and more.

🚫 Troubleshooting

Why am I seeing no results after applying filters?

If no results show, try removing or relaxing some filters. Too many filters can return empty results.

What should I do if the results page doesn't load?

Make sure your browser allows redirects and JavaScript. Refresh the page or try disabling strict privacy settings.

Why do filters return different results than expected on Amazon?

Amazon occasionally updates or re-ranks listings. Results may vary slightly based on timing or region.

Can I search using ASIN, ISBN, or UPC?

Yes! In Step 1, you can enter a keyword or use an ASIN, ISBN, or UPC for precise targeting.

How do I search Amazon for in-stock items under $20?

Use Step 2 to set a price max of $20 and Step 5 to enable the “In Stock” filter. Deal Shopper Pro applies this before you even visit Amazon.

Can I find deals from Amazon’s own brands?

Yes. Just enable the “Amazon Brands” checkbox in Step 5.

Are refurbished or warehouse deals included?

Yes! You can filter specifically for “Warehouse & Refurbished” products in Step 5.

How do I find niche ethical products (eco, vegan, cruelty-free)?

Enable Step 5 filters like ♻️ Eco-Friendly, 🌱 Vegan, 🐰 Cruelty-Free, 📶 Low EMF, and more — all in one search.

Is this powered by AI or automation?

Yes — Deal Shopper Pro uses automation to generate filtered search links. It also offers a voice input button on supported browsers. No AI is used for tracking or profiling.
