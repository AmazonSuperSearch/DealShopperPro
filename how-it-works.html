How to Use Deal Shopper Pro in 5 Easy Steps

  1. Go to the homepage.
  2. Step 1: Enter a keyword (e.g., headphones, yoga mat).
  3. Step 2: Select your price range (optional).
  4. Step 3: Choose a discount percentage, minimum customer rating, and how you'd like your results sorted (optional).
  5. Step 4: Want results from specific brands only? Type the brand name and check "Match only these brands" (optional).
  6. Step 5: Check any additional filters you'd like. You can select multiple options, but note that too many filters may result in no matches (optional).
  7. Step 6: Click the blue "View Filtered Results" button to see your results on Amazon.
